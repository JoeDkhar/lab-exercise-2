What is climate change?

Climate change is a change in the usual weather patterns in a region over time. It affects the global climate system and can have significant impacts on natural and human systems. Learn more about its causes, effects, and what we can do to address it.

How do we know the world’s climate is changing?

The world’s average temperature has changed throughout history due to various natural factors. However, recent changes are largely driven by human activities, such as burning fossil fuels and deforestation, which increase greenhouse gases in the atmosphere.

How can we tackle this problem?

Addressing climate change requires coordinated efforts at local, national, and global levels. Actions include reducing greenhouse gas emissions, transitioning to renewable energy sources, and implementing policies to protect natural ecosystems. Every individual can contribute by adopting sustainable practices and supporting climate-friendly initiatives.
